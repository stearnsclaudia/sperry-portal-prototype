Route Verification

Commercial trucks are assigned as either Nonstop Route or Stop & Verify Route.

Nonstop Route

Verifier-linked workflow

Chief verifies how many pictures were taken each day. The Field Manager is automatically notified when the Chief submits the picture count. That night, the linked Verifier is notified. The next day, the Field Manager is automatically notified again once the Verifier confirms they checked all pictures.

Stop & Verify Route

Chief verifies defects directly

Chief confirms how many defects they found themselves. The Field Manager is automatically notified daily of the defect count entered by the Chief. No separate Verifier picture-confirmation step unless assigned by the Field Manager.
